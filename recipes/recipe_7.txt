Delux pizza

3 flour
10 pepperoni
2 tomato sauce
3 cheese
1 onion
3 bacon
3 mushrooms

Mix water with flour and yeast until is soft and consistent.
Let dough rise for 2 hours, then knead the dough for 10 minutes.
Stretch dough into large circle and lay tomato sauce uniformly on pizza.
Sprinkle shredded cheese on pizza, then uniformly add sliced onions, grilled bacon, sliced mushroom, and sliced pepperoni.
Cook pizza in oven pre-heated at 400 degrees F.
