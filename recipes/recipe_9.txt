Ratatouille

1 eggplant
2 tomato
1 zucchini
1 yellow zucchini

Cut all vegetables into circular slices.
Gather off-cuts and simmer on medium-low heat, season with salt and pepper.
Smash simmer into paste and smear onto the bottom of a baking tray.
Place slices of vegetables in alternating pattern around the bottom of the tray.
Bake at 350 degrees F.
