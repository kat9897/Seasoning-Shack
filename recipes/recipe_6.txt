Steak dinner

4 beef
4 mushrooms
1 onion
1 potato

Season beef with oil, salt and pepper.
On high heat, lay the beef on the pan.
After 3 minutes, flip beef.
After 3 minutes, put beef in oven preheadted at 400 degrees F.
In seperate pot, boil water.
Add potato to boil for 3 minutes.
Remove potatoes and mash until fine.
On medium heat, fry sliced onions and mushrooms.
Plate and pour gravy from beef onto the mashed potatoes.
