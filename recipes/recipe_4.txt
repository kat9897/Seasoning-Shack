Spaghetti and meatballs

2 pasta
2 tomatoes
3 beef
1 cheese
1 onion

Boil pasta in a pot of water.
In an seperate pan, add oil and fry diced onions and tomatoes.
Season onions and tomatoes with salt pepper, and add pasta water.
Add beef balls to the sauce and cook until seared.
Add pasta to the sauce and stur until uniformed.
Shred cheese on top of pasta.
