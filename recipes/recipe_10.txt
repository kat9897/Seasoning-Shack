Sushi

1 cucumber
1 salmon
2 rice
1 seaweed
1 pickled carrot
1 bell pepper

Lay out seaweed on top of sushi roller.
Layer rice on seaweed.
cut salmon, bell pepper, and cucumber julian.
Lay out sliced salmon, bell peper, cucumber, and pickled carrots uniformly on the rice laternaly.
Roll using the sushi roller, pressing tight to firm up the sushi roll.
Cut at uniform intervals and serve with wassabi.
