Beef noodle soup 

2 noodles
1 egg
3 choy
4 beef
2 spring onions
1 chili pepper
2 garlics

Sear 4 cubed beef at medium-high heat.
Add 2 crushed garlic and 1 chili pepper, with salt and soy sauce.
In another pot, boil water, then add noodles. Making sure noodles submerged.
After 2 minutes, add beef and choy.
After 2 minutes, crack into the pot, an egg.
Cook until noodles are slightly translucent.
sprinkle cut spring onions on top to finish.
