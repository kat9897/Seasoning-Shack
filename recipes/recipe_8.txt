BLT burger

4 ground meat
1 burger bun
1 cheese
2 lettuce
1 tomato
1 onion

Season patty with salt and pepper.
Oil pan and sear patty, 2 minutes on each side.
Melt cheese on patty.
Toast bun in the burger grease.
Assemble with mayo, lettuce, patty with cheese, onion, tomato, mayo, bun.
