Grilled salmon

2 butter
2 thyme
6 asparagus
1 salmon
2 garlic
1 lemon 

Make small cuts on the skin side of the salmon, season with salt and pepper.
On medium-low heat, melt 2 butter.
before butter is melted completely, place salmon on pan, skin side down.
Add to the pan, 2 thyme and 2 crushed garlic.
Cook for 2 minutes, and flip the salmon over.
Add the asparagus to the pan and shut the lid.
Cook for 2 minutes, and squeeze lemon juice on salmon.
